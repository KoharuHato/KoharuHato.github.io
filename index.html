Quick Tutorial Guide

Hello! I'm glad your here. On the very top of the screen, you will see a navigation bar with options of what you need help with: practice, productivity, motivation, and habit tracker. Try clicking on one of these icons. After that, you will see a little hamburger icon that looks like this: ☰ on the top left of your screen. Click on the hamburger icon (☰) to see the available options within that section. Have fun and enjoy your new productivity app!
